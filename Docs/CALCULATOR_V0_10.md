# Excel Calculator v0.10

File:

`Excel/Line-Energy-Solar-Calculator-v0.10.xlsx`

## Added in v0.10

- `CableSizing` sheet with starter copper cable ampacity assumptions.
- Recommended DC cable section.
- DC voltage-drop estimate.
- Suggested DC string fuse rating based on panel short-circuit current.
- Estimated AC current from inverter output power and phase type.
- Recommended AC cable section.
- AC voltage-drop estimate.
- Suggested AC breaker rating.
- Estimated battery current from inverter output power and nominal battery voltage.
- Recommended battery cable section.
- Suggested battery breaker rating.
- Cable sizing outputs added to `Summary` and `Equipment`.

## Current Limitation

These are preliminary sizing outputs for project planning. Final cable sections, fuses, breakers, voltage-drop limits, installation method, derating factors, selectivity, SPD type, and earthing system must be verified against local electrical standards and equipment manuals.
