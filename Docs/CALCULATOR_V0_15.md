# Excel Calculator v0.15

File:

`Excel/Line-Energy-Solar-Calculator-v0.15.xlsx`

## Added in v0.15

- `MonthlyGeneration` sheet.
- `MonthlyYieldProfile` sheet with starter regional monthly yield distribution.
- Monthly generation for the Standard option.
- Monthly consumption comparison.
- Monthly self-consumed energy.
- Monthly exported energy.
- Monthly self-consumption value.
- Monthly export / green tariff value.
- Monthly total value and coverage percentage.
- Summary outputs for best month, worst month, yearly self-consumption value, and yearly export value.

## Current Limitation

Monthly profiles are starter seasonal assumptions marked `seed_verify_source`. For real proposals, replace them with project-specific PVGIS, Global Solar Atlas, or measured local yield data.
