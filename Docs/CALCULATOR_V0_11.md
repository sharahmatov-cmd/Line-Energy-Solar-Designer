# Excel Calculator v0.11

File:

`Excel/Line-Energy-Solar-Calculator-v0.11.xlsx`

## Added in v0.11

- Inputs for installation method, cable grouping derating, ambient temperature derating, lightning protection condition, and AC earthing system.
- `SpdRules` sheet with starter SPD selection guidance.
- `EarthingRules` sheet with starter earthing-system checklist notes.
- `CableDerating` sheet with starter cable derating assumptions.
- SPD recommendation output in `Summary`, `CableProtection`, and `Equipment`.
- Earthing system output in `Summary`, `CableProtection`, and `Equipment`.
- Combined cable derating factor used in DC, AC, and battery cable sizing formulas.
- Derated DC cable ampacity output.

## Current Limitation

This is still a preliminary design assistant. Final SPD type, SPD coordination, earthing system, RCD requirements, cable derating, and protective-device selectivity must be checked against local electrical standards, utility requirements, inverter manuals, battery manuals, and site conditions.
