# Excel Calculator v0.9

File:

`Excel/Line-Energy-Solar-Calculator-v0.9.xlsx`

## Added in v0.9

- `CableProtection` sheet.
- `ProtectionRules` sheet.
- Inputs for DC cable route length, AC cable route length, and grounding cable route length.
- Starter quantities for PV DC cables, PE cable, DC string fuses, DC isolator, DC SPD, AC breaker, AC SPD, AC cable, battery DC cable pair, and battery fuse/breaker.
- Protection items added to the `Equipment` sheet.
- Protection item count added to the `Summary` sheet.

## Current Limitation

The module currently estimates quantities only. Cable sections, fuse ratings, breaker ratings, SPD type, selectivity, and earthing details must be selected according to local electrical standards and equipment manuals.
