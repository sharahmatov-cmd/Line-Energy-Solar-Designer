# Battery Module

The battery module stores supported battery models and provides a first capacity calculation in the Excel calculator.

## Current Brands

- Pylontech
- Dyness
- Deye ESS
- Gobel Power
- GSL Energy
- Felicity ESS
- HinaESS
- Basen Green
- HTE
- SRNE ESS
- Hinen
- Svolt ESS
- EVE Energy ESS

## Current Excel Outputs

- Selected battery model
- Battery nominal energy
- Battery quantity
- Total nominal battery energy
- Compatibility note

## Data Status

Records with `seed_verify_datasheet` contain starter values for development but still need datasheet verification before commercial use.

Records with `model_only_needs_datasheet` are catalog placeholders. Electrical limits, charge/discharge current, exact usable energy, communication settings, and warranty data must be filled from official datasheets.
